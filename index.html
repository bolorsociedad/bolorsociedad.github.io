Guildore [FINANCE]

Guildore is an online technical analysis tool. The many features Guildore offer include:

  • Stock's volume each day.
  • Switch from candlestick and line charts.
  • A number of technical analysis indicators:
    • Moving Average
    • Exponential Moving Average
    • Bollinger Bands
    • Standard Deviation
    • Average True Range (ATR)
    • MACD
  • Add stock to watchlist.
  • Save stocks in a portfolio, and track their performance.
The GitHub repository is available here, and a live version of Guildore is available at http://www.guildore.com.

Space Journey [GAME]

Your objective is simple: travel from planet to planet, get 3 stars and reach the portal to complete the levels. Sounds easy, right?

Well, it's not. You will have to be both clever and skillful in order to overcome the difficulties that gravity causes, as well as the danger that asteroids and black holes create.

You can unlock special beautiful costumes too for your character. But not only will those costumes make you look cuter, but they will also provide you special powers: low gravity, super jump, inverse gravity...
