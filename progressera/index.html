Progresser

Progresser is a minimalist progress bar made in JavaScript. This is a demo of a page that uses Progresser.
Progresser.start() – Shows the progress bar
Progresser.set(0.75) – Sets a percentage
Progresser.inc() – Increments by a little.
Progresser.done() – Completes the progress.
